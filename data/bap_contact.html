About Us

Big Apple Recreational Sports is New York City’s non-profit, community-minded co-ed sports league for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer and LGBTQI-friendly New Yorkers who want to play competitive sports in an athletic environment free of harassment, discrimination and drama. Our leagues are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all participants.

BEHIND THE SCENES

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a community of players that extends beyond the lanes, courts, and fields.

Our Story

A league created and run by volunteers from our community.
